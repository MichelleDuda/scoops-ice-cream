About Us

Scoops is an artisan ice cream shop based in Beale, County Kerry. We specialize in unique flavor combinations, handcrafted in small batches, using fresh local ingredients. Our ice cream is free from artificial colorings, flavorings, and preservatives.


Here at Scoops we craft every batch with a fine attention to detail that will hopefully leave you saying “Wow! That was the best ice cream I have ever had!”
